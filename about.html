About E-Waste Hub ♻️

Hey there! We’re just a bunch of caffeine-fueled students trying to save the planet, one circuit board at a time. 😎

This platform was born from a single thought: "Why does recycling feel like rocket science?" So, we made it simple. You can now find recycling centers, schedule pickups, and learn cool eco-hacks — all in one place. 🌱

Our mission is to make e-waste recycling as easy as ordering your favorite food online (minus the guilt). 😅

🌟 Fun Facts:

  • Yes, we broke a few laptops making this.
  • Half the team runs on coffee, the other half on sheer panic.
  • Our platform is greener than your neighbor’s lawn (no offense to anyone).

So yeah... stick around, explore, recycle a little, and maybe laugh while you’re at it. We won’t mind 😉
